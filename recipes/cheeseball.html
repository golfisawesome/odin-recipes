Diana's Holiday Cheese Ball

About this recipe

This is a family recipe by our cousin Dianna Churchman. Passed down to her by her family. It goes great with ritz crackers and perfect for the next holiday get together.

Ingredients

  • 3 8 oz pkgs of cream cheese, I prefer Kraft
  • 5 Green Onions Chopped
  • 1 Small Jar Dried Beef or the Budding Thin Beef
  • 2 Cups Chopped Pecans

    • Instructions

    1. Cut dried beef and green onions into small pieces in a bowl. Mix cream cheese, dried beef, and green onions
    2. Cover and chill for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight
    3. About an hour before serving, form mixture into a ball and roll in pecans
    4. Serve with crackers
